动态刷新
config_client加入pom.xml
<dependency>
	<groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
	<artifactId>spring-boot-starter-actuator</artifactId>
</dependency>

向客户端发送post请求
http://127.0.0.1:7002/refresh

然后再查看(值已改变)
